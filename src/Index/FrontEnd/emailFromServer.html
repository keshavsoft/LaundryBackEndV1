For those that value kindness, Random Acts of Kindness Week encourages people out there to be loud about how easy it is to give to others. This holiday centers around making kindness a standard for others to follow to make society a better place to live in.

Kind ideas for this week

GIVE COMPLIMENTS

If you're meeting a friend or family, take some time to notice something nice about them and tell them all about it!

SEND A KIND NOTE

If you haven't talked with a friend for a long time, remind them that you still care. Send them a text, or even better, write them a letter.

SURPRISE A NEIGHBOUR

What a better way to show some love than with bake goods! Let them know you appreciate them

SMILE AT STRANGERS

Riding with someone in the elevator? Sharing a table at a work space? Smile!

DONATE TO A SHELTER

Kindness is for everyone! Take this chance to give to those need kindness the most.

BE KIND TO YOURSELF

Sometimes we give a lot and we forget about ourselves. Take some time for stuff you love!
